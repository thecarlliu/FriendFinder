Who are the Snelling Scots?

The Snelling Scots are a branch of the merry tribe known as the Fighting Scots. Long ago, they branched off from the main tribe and set out, leaving the sacred dormitories at Macalester College, in pursuit of new lands to divide and conquer. Eventually, they found a suitable habitat on Snelling Ave and claimed it as their own. To this day, their lands are still being passed down from one generation to the next, as the older Snelling Scots leave to pursue new adventures and ambitions, and younger Fighting Scots take the next step into adulthood and inherit their predecessors' domain.
